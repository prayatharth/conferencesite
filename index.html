About the Conference

ICAITE is organized by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, United Institute of Technology,Prayagraj,U.P,India . The conference is technically sponsored by ELSEVIER . Uttar Pradesh Section is located in Region 10 and is represented at the India Council. IEEE UP Section interfaces with industries and academia through various technical and humanitarian activities. Conference Proceedings will be abstracted and indexed by IEEE Xplore.

The conference will provide an opportunity to the practicing engineers, academicians, and researchers to meet in a forum to discuss various issues and its future direction of various Emerging Technologies of allied areas of Industry applications with the following outcome:

  • To strengthen the industry-Academia bond and enhance collaborative and meaningful research on issues of current relevance.
  • Expose the participating delegates to the emerging technologies through presentations.
  • To organize expert’s talk by domain experts of global eminence.
  • To recognize the eminent global achievers for their remarkable achievement and contributions in their respective domains.

The conference will cover a wide range of topics related to electrical, electronics, and computing technologies, including but not limited to:

Computing Technologies

  • Artificial intelligence
  • Deep learning
  • Machine learning and prompt engineering
  • Cyber Physical system
  • Software Engineering and Agile Development
  • Multimedia and Web Services Agents
  • Cloud Computing
  • Big Data and quantum computing
  • Ubiquitous Computing
  • Global Software Engineering
  • Internet of Things
  • Knowledge-based Systems
  • Pervasive Computing and Ambient Intelligence
  • Bioinformatics
  • Heuristic and AI Planning Strategies and Tools
  • Computational Theories of Learning
  • Recent Trends and Developments

ABOUT UNITED INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

The United Institute of Technology (UIT) established in the year 2007 under the aegis of Shri Shiv Ram Das Gulati foundation. UIT offers bachelor of technology program in the field of Computer Science, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication and also M.Tech. in Computer Science and Electronics.

UIT with a vision “To be a value based institute of academic excellence and research by fostering individuals for their career and life” wish to achieve higher standard and quality of education. The college provides its students with academic, extra-curricular and other necessary facilities to groom them into corporate leaders.

The team has dedicated and well qualified faculty members to facilitate high standard of teaching & learning process. These faculty members are also involved in research work attaining research publications in repute international conferences and journals.


ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Computer science and engineering department of United Institute of Technology committed to offer quality education, so that the students are able to solve complex problems. The department aspires to achieve the standard established by various quality education promoting organization like NBA, NIRF, ARIA etc.

The department provides advanced level courses in Big Data, Data Analytics, Image Processing, Block chain, Parallel and Distributed Computing, Data Warehousing and Mining etc. Department initiates every year several industrial training, foreign travels, expert talks etc. to make students actively engage in the recent advances in the field of engineering. The department encourages students to develop new idea and promote entrepreneurship.
